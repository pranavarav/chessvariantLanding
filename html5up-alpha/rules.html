Rules

Explore the Unique Game Mechanics of Asymmetric Tempo.

Gameplay Overview

  • White starts with 1 move, Black responds with 2 moves
  • Players alternate in a sequence of 2 moves (White) and 2 moves (Black), and so on.

Key Rules

  • Piece Movement: Each move must use different pieces. A piece cannot move twice in the same turn.

  • Check Resolution:
    • First Move Results in Check:
      • The opponent must resolve the check with their first move.
      • The checking player retains full two moves.
      • The normal turn sequence continues.
    • Second Move Results in Check:
      • The opponent must resolve the check on their next turn's first move.
      • The opponent's second move on their next turn follows normally, using a different piece than the first move.
  • Captures and Special Moves: Follow standard chess rules, with castling counting as one move.

Game End

  • Standard chess checkmate rules apply. The game ends when a king is in an unresolvable check.
